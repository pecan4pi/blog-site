2026/5/20 thoughts; poetry & trauma

Hi there, it's been a while. I'm 15 now & almost done with highschool. Got my boaters license & have driven a boat on Lake Erie twice (not my boat). Ended up beating most of my stage fright somehow, & made a ton of friends at highschool. Also got a new new 3ds xl (mhx edition) modded, & started playing competitive splatoon 3. prolly do another blog soonish about all that.

Either way, lets get to the subject of this blog; poetry & trauma. I'll start with trauma first

So, I have it pretty good when it comes to family, parents & my household. I have very supporting parents. We have a stable income & a nice house. Life is generally good, but a lot of times I hear people talking about how I'm lucky & how they have awful parents who are sorta cruel(?) for a lack of a better word. I sort of took that all for granted, but today I finally understand what everyone is talking about. So I was on a call with a friend playing mc, & he had to afk to help out with something. The shit I heard is actually insane to me. There was a whole bunch of cursing from the adults & everyone was really fucking agressive. At one point, his mother said something along the lines of "you don't pay the fucking bills around here so keep the water on fucking cold." My friend sounded really guilty when he appologised. A little bit later there was a whole bunch of yelling & cursing while presumably a 300 pound water heater I think. Everyone sounded unhappy & just in general, all that I heard made me understand. Understand the "house trauma" & the bad parents. I get it now.


A few days ago I finally understood poetry, it just clicked. So in ELA we had a short poetry unit, with there being a "poetry slam" at the end of it (ended up being 2026/5/20 I think). I ended up writing a poem about boats, & read it to the school at the poetry slam. People thought it was pretty good & I was pretty proud of it. I also heard a ton of great poems written by my peers, which sorta has me slightly inspired for a lack of a better word.
Either way, I'm now thirsty for writing some more poems. I'm prolly gonna make a poetry website for any poems that I make. I could also make a new main website because "pecan4pi.github.io/website" is way too chaotic & old for me to do anything with it without destroying a really good time capsule of myself about a month after after I started 8th grade (& 8th grade is very near & dear to my heart, as it's probably my favorite year & definitely the one I look back on with the most nostalgia). The final option would to just put my poems here or something, but I don't know if I want to do that. I'm quite rusty with html so I kinda want to go dust offthe old html 5 guidebook from the 2000's & make another page from scratch. Either way, I think I finally like poetry, which is a day I though I would never see.

That wraps up this blog thingy. Drink water (don't fall for those damn moisturizing products or extra electrolyte sports drinks, just drink water!), Remember that life will get better even if its difficult right now, & I hope you have a great rest of your day. ♥
